COVID-19: A Visual Essay

This visual essay will narrate the timeline of the COVID-19 pandemic around the world, and the effect the vaccines had on the scale of the outbreak.

Firstly, we will examine how the pandemic spread across different countries and continents from its onset. Next, we will see which countries most successfully managed the pandemic. We will also investigate whether there is a correlation between the relative wealth of a country and the spread of the pandemic. Additionally, we will look at the impact of vaccinations on the spread of cases and deaths, and whether booster jabs also affected the spread and transmissibility of the virus. Finally, we will consider how the geographical position of a country might have influenced the impact of the pandemic on them.

How did the pandemic spread across the world from the onset?

Cases initially started appearing around the 5th of January 2020 in China, Germany and Finland.

On February 1st, the total cases was 12K and appearing in Western Europe, North America, and Southeast Asia.

On April 1st, the total cases was 894K with Europe growing the most rapidly.

On September 1st, the total cases was around 26M with Asia now growing the fastest.

By February 1st 2021, total cases globally exceeded 100M with Europe and North America growing the fastest.

On August 1st, total cases are about to exceed 200M with the fastest growing.
